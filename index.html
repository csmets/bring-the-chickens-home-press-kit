Pitch

Fight against the clock as you gather as many chickens you can and bring them home. Whilst being challenged by an endless wave of varying predators. Convert your following chickens into an army to push them back. Play in single player or work together in local co-op.

Description

Your chickens need help! They're lost from their adventurous day out and you need to bring them home fast. Kills hundreds of angry predators that are hungry to eat your chickens and be wary of incoming traffic. This mayhem filled action game has a bunch of exciting levels that test your survival skills. You can play solo or team up with a friend on local co-op.

Key features:
- A hectic fun action game with roguelite elements!!
- A selection of maps where you must try to survive as long as you can and save as many chickens as you can.
- Speed, guns, explosion, cannons and many more with the use of power ups!
- Compete against other times online and get a WORLD RECORD!
- Endless fun exploring different strategies to try and score your best times.
